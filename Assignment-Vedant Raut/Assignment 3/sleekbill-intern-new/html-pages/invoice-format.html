Detailed Format of a Tax Invoice



To comprehend the structure better, let's delve into the specific components of a standard tax invoice:

Heading:

Document heading to understand type of document.

Logo:

Your companies of brand logo on invoice.

Details of Supplier & Buyer:

Addresses and names of both Supplier and Buyer involved.

GSTIN:

GST numbers for both buyer and supplier.

Key Dates:

The issue date (creation date) and the due date.

Place of Supply:

Indicates the location where goods/services are delivered.

Bill To & Ship To:

Detailed information about the buyer and the shipping destination.

Table of Sales Perticular:

Tabulated data capturing item details, HSN code, unit of materials, quantity, taxable value, taxes, and total amounts, and transport charges, TDS/ TCS charges if applicable.

Bank Details:

Bank to receive payment from customer bank via payment mode NEFT, RTGS & IMPS.

Terms and Conditions:

Any specific conditions or term for ore and post condition of the sale.

Signature and Stamp:

Authenticating the document, ensuring it's officially recognized.

UPI QR Code:

UPI QR code to collect payment faster than ever.
