What is a Sales Invoice?


A sales invoice is a formal document issued by a seller to a buyer, providing details:

Types of Sales Invoices


  • Proforma Invoice : A preliminary bill sent before delivery of goods/services to provide an estimated cost breakdown.

  • Standard Invoice : The most commonly used invoice for requesting payments in small businesses.

  • Credit Invoice : Issued when a refund or discount is applied. It reflects a negative total, reducing the amount given.

  • Debit Invoice : Used when additional services or charges are added to an existing transaction.

  • Commercial Invoice : Used for international sales, providing details on cross-border transactions for customs clearance.
