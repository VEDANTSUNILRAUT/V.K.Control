Frequently asked questions




How safe is my data?

We use the best security encryption to make sure your data is safely stored and transmitted over the internet. Moreover, having your data in Sleek Bill Online means that when your computer crashes, your data is still there, where you can easily access it any time you want.

Does it also work offline?

While Sleek Bill Online only works with an active internet connection, we do have another product, Sleek Bill for Desktop, that is a great alternative if you wish to do your invoicing offline. It also has a free version and some paid ones, contact us for more details.

Does it have GST support?

Yes! Sleek Bill Online is fully compliant now with GST now. Among the features we have : GSTIN support, HSN/SAC CODE, GST tax slabs, calculation for CGST, SGST and IGST cess and many more features. We're constantly working on it and release updates every month.

Do you have option for multiple users

Yes. A new plan was launched with all features in Premium plan plus Unlimited users with various permission levels, Unlimited documents and a few upcoming new reports and features. The multi-user feature allows you to invite as many users as you want and set their permission level to anything from Only sales to Only Purchasing or just option to view and download.

Do I have to install any software?

No, Sleek Bill Online is a 100% web based software, you don't need to install anything on your computer. You can access it from any computer, PC or MAC, or even from your tablet. For security reasons, we do recommend that you access Sleek Bill Online only from trusted devices. .

I don't have access to internet all the time, do you have offline software?

Yes, we do! Sleek Bill for Desktop is a great alternative to Sleek Bill Online. You can use it to make invoices, quotes, delivery notes, credit notes, purchase orders, bills, track stock and we also have some great inventory and sales reports. Download the free trial here.

Why should I choose Sleek Bill Online?

Sleek Bill Online was made specifically for SMBs in India, therefore you can be sure that everything is according to the current laws. It has all the features necessary for smooth invoicing and it comes with a clean, easy to use interface.
