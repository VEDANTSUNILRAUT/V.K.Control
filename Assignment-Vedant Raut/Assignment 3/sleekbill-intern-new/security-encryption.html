Will you steal my data?

Security is in one of our main concerns and we take all possible steps to ensure your data's security.

Here at Sleek Bill Online we really value our customer's privacy. We know that many of you rely on us for a big part of your business, billing and stock maintenance, so we really take your needs seriously. That is why, the security of our software, systems and data are our number one priority.

Security

All information that is transmitted between your browser and Sleek Bill online is protected with 256-bit SSl encryption. You've probably seen a lock icon in your browser, next to the app's address, which verifies that you are not using a phishing site that is impersonating Sleek Bill. This is proof that your data is secure in transit.

Firewalls

Sleek Bill Online and all the data you have entered in it securely sits behind Amazon AWS firewalls, configured by Engine Yard. This system is used by some of the biggest companies in the world and is widely acknowledged as safe and secure.

IP Spoofing

Engine Yard instances are unable to send spoofed network traffic. The AWS-controlled firewall infrastructure will not permit an instance to send traffic with a source IP or MAC address other than its own.

Strong encryption

Sleek Bill Online's servers are safely located in state of the art datacenters that use multiple levels of security like biometric access controls, constant surveillance and carefully monitored climate control to protect the servers that store your data.

Reliability

Redundant servers and data centers Sleek Bill Online's infrastructure uses redundant storage and servers to keep the application and data available in case there is any hardware failure and has another set of servers and storage in a different datacentre, in case our primary one is unavailable due to a disaster or other disruption.
