Services Of Cattle Hub

Your trusted marketplace for premium farming and dairy cattle.

Explore Services

Shen-Khat (Organic Manure Production)

Transform cattle dung into high-quality organic manure or bio-fertilizer, improving soil health and increasing crop yield. This eco-friendly service helps farmers reduce chemical fertilizer dependency while generating additional income.

Cattle Health & Veterinary Care

Ensure your cattle’s well-being with expert veterinary services, including regular health check-ups, vaccinations, AI-based disease detection, and emergency medical care. Healthy cattle lead to better productivity and profitability.

Cattle Feeding & Nutrition Management

Get access to scientifically balanced diet plans, premium quality fodder, and nutritional supplements to enhance cattle growth, milk production, and overall health. Optimize feeding strategies for maximum efficiency.

Online Cattle Marketplace & Management

Buy and sell cattle with ease using our online platform. Access training resources, monitor cattle health in real-time, and connect with experts to improve your farm’s efficiency and profitability.

Cattle Trust & Insurance Services

Protect your investment with cattle insurance and financial aid programs. Our cooperative trust services ensure financial security in case of cattle loss, illness, or other unforeseen events.
